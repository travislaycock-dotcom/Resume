Ergonomic Assembly Cart / Fixture

Role: Mechanical design lead — concept to shop‑floor build.

Problem: Operators needed a safe way to align and handle components without strain and with quick changeovers.

Constraints: Limited footprint, EHS compliance, adjustable reach, quick‑release features, standard pneumatics.

Approach: Developed modular roller arms, pneumatic assist, and locking positions; routed cables and air lines for safety.

Outcome: Repeatable positioning and improved ergonomics; simplified maintenance via accessible fasteners and guards.

Precision Roller Fixture

Role: Designer and test developer.

Problem: Inconsistent press‑fit and runout measurements created scrap and rework.

Approach: Machined baseplate with interchangeable nests, gauge blocks, and positive stops; documented step‑by‑step work instructions.

Outcome: Faster setup and consistent measurement; improved quality checks before assembly.
